# smart-on-fhir
This application is an humble beginning for our team to understand the smart on fhir standard in a nav 
context. The purpose of the app is to learn and to share our knowledge at the topic.

## Efforts
We have tried different approaches to learn about FHIR and [SMART on FHIR](https://docs.smarthealthit.org/) 
to prepare for a wider adoption in the Norwegian market.
